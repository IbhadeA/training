About Easter Bunny School

At Easter Bunny School everything we do revolves around our key drivers: Opportunity, Diversity, Curiosity and Tenacity. Through this focus, our children explore challenges and embrace all the new challenges they meet with confidence and a positive attitude.

Our aim is for our children to be confident, caring, resilient, curious young adults with a desire to learn and achieve the best they can in all they do and be best prepared for the future. Our children are wonderful ambassadors, so if you would like to know more about what makes our school the place that it is, please get in touch and our children we would be delighted to show you around and introduce you to everyone who makes Easter Bunny School such a special and incredible place.

About Mrs Cottontail - Head of School

Petra joined Easter Bunny School as a Newly Qualified Teacher in 1998. She moved to develop her skills further in a special school in 2005, then moved back to a large, urban mainstream school as a member of the Senior Leadership Team, firstly as Year Leader, then as Assistant Headteacher and as Acting Deputy Headteacher, before re-joining Easter Bunny School in April 2016 as Assistant Headteacher. Since then she has been appointed as Head of School.

The school has always been special to her, with her two children having attended the school. Petra is passionate about education and ensuring each and every child has the opportunity to shine and reach their potential, irrespective of any challenges along the way.
